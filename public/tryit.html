Change school: try our latest projects


We’re looking for high schoolers who are bored, stressed, hungry, or unhappy in school (so… all of you).

Help us test Doorstops, digital resources that help students change school! Think Khan Academy for being happier in class, feeling safer in the hallways, and ending toxic culture schoolwide.

There will be free donuts.

We’ll meet, chat, and you’ll get the chance to try personalized strategies that help you navigate school. That might be an email template for getting an extension on an assignment, a cash grant to buy a waffle-maker and cook for your friends who skip lunch, or a playbook on using cutting-edge behavioral psych research to end hyper-competitive culture at your school.


You don't have to feel trapped at your desk


I spent the past year traveling to schools in 15 states sitting in on classes, eating um... lunches, and asking students what's broken about school.

If an FBI hostage negotiator had your worst teacher, or Gandhi had to fight your school's most toxic policy, or that hot dog eating contest guy had to eat your school lunch... okay he probably would, that's not the point:

If sharp, capable people were in your shoes, would they feel trapped? Stressed? Anxious?

Would they do anything differently?

My job is to convince you the answers are no, no, no, and yes, then to show you how to change school.
