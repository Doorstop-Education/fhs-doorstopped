Start an internship program for seniors

Cold EmailingNetworkingPower Plays

Everybody talks about how school doesn't teach you real life skills. Why not start a program in school that does?

This doorstop is in beta-mode, meaning we're helping student groups at a few schools go through the process by hand to learn how to do it.

Wanna join? Email ben@fix.school with a little information about your school and a sentence on why you think a program like this would be awesome.

Goal: turn the last month of senior year into an internship program where every student goes and gets a month of work experience.

First step: beta-test the program with 10-20 seniors in July, the first month after they graduate, or wit 10-20 juniors in August, before they start senior year.
